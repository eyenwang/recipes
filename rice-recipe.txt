FRIED RICE WITH EGG, HAM, AND PEAS

INGREDIENTS

3 cup previously cooked rice, refridgerated
1 scallion, green and white portions thinly sliced
1/2 cup chopped ham
1 egg, beaten
1/2 cup frozen peas
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup oil

1. Heat a wok on high heat, until a drop of water sizzles and evaporates immediately.
2. Add 2 tablespoon oil to wok.
3. Stir in egg into wok, and allow to cook for 10 secs; stir until uncooked portions solidify.  As soon as egg is done scrape into into a bowl. Use a fork to cut up egg into small pieces.
4. Take wok off heat and wipe out with paper towel.
5. Reheat wok as above.
6. Add remainder of oil.
7. Quickly cook scallions until fragrant.
8. Add rice, using spatula or wooden spoon to break up grains and to ensure coating with oil.
9. Cook until rice is fragrant, stirring constantly, approxiately 5 minutes.  Add salt, ham, and peas, and cook for 2 minutes longer.
10.  Add egg and stir into rice.
11.  Serve immediately into bowls.
